# WatchBuddy — Account Deletion

You can delete your WatchBuddy account and all data associated with it at any
time. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

## In the app (instant)

1. Open WatchBuddy, go to the **Profile** tab and tap **Edit Profile**.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete account…**
3. Confirm twice. Your profile, library, watch history, ratings, reviews, favorites
   and follows are erased and you are signed out.

## By request

If you can no longer access the app, open an issue at
[github.com/RobertJmek/WatchBuddy/issues](https://github.com/RobertJmek/WatchBuddy/issues)
from any account, or email the address on the developer's GitHub profile,
stating the email you signed up with. Deletion requests are honored within
30 days.

## What is deleted

Everything tied to your account: sign-in identity (email), profile (name,
username, bio, avatar), library entries, watch history, ratings, reviews,
favorites and follow relationships. Nothing is retained afterwards.
